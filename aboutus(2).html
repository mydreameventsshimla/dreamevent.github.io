About Us

Destination Wedding in Shimla is your luxury wedding partner for dream ceremonies nestled in nature. With over 7+ years of experience in the wedding industry, we bring your love story to life in a way that's elegant, effortless, and unforgettable.

From intimate hilltop vows to grand palace celebrations, our team of expert planners, designers, and hospitality pros manages it all – decor, venue, catering, photography, entertainment, and more. Every couple we serve becomes part of our extended family.

We have hosted over 250+ weddings across destinations like Shimla, Manali, Udaipur, Rishikesh, Goa, and Kerala – delivering not just events, but experiences that stay etched in hearts forever.

Our passion is to craft bespoke celebrations that reflect your culture, style, and personality. We believe weddings should be stress-free, joyful, and magical – and we take pride in delivering that promise every time.

Let’s begin your forever with beauty, warmth, and unforgettable charm.

Because your love story deserves a fairytale setting.
